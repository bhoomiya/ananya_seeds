Dr. Lakshmikant Pandey

Managing Director & CEO | "The Seedman"

A Legacy Rooted in Excellence

Dr. Lakshmikant Pandey's 35+ year journey in India's seed industry reflects an unwavering commitment to agricultural innovation and farmer prosperity. As the founder and Managing Director of Ananya Seeds since 2014, he has transformed the company into a trusted name for quality vegetable and field crop seeds across India.

Academic Foundation

  • PhD in Botany (Microbial Biotechnology)
  • MSc in Botany (Plant Physiology)
  • MBA from DDU Gorakhpur University
  • UGC NET Qualified (1984)

Professional Journey

  • 1990-1997: Management Trainee to Regional Manager, Mahyco
  • 1997-2012: Production & National Business, Sungro Seeds
  • 2012-2013: GM Government Sales, Mahyco
  • 2014-Present: Founder & MD, Ananya Seeds

Areas of Expertise

  • Hybrid seed production & quality assurance
  • Sustainable & climate-resilient agriculture
  • Export-import & international trade
  • Rural marketing & farmer empowerment
  • Supply chain & business development
  • Government relations & subsidy operations

Industry Leadership

  • National Vice-President, Indian Industries Association
  • Advisor, Patanjali Organic Research Institute
  • Advisor, Advanta Seeds
  • VP Sustainable Agriculture, Ekal Gramothan Foundation
  • Industry Specialist, Emkay Global Financial Services
  • Visiting Faculty, IIM Lucknow

Awards & Honors

  • AIMA-Dr. J.S. Juneja Award
  • CII Industrial Innovation Award (2015)
  • RMAI Flame Asia Award
  • RMAI's Rural Marketing Leadership Award (2017)
  • "50 Most Influential Rural Marketing Professionals"
  • Recognitions from IARI, HAU-Hisar, Pantnagar University

Philosophy & Vision

Dr. Pandey believes in a farmer-first approach that integrates technology with tradition:

Technology for Good: Enhancing taste, nutrition, and crop resilience
Farmer Empowerment: Ensuring fair prices for sustained prosperity
Sustainable Solutions: Developing climate-resilient crops
Global Thinking: Indian brand with international standards

Thought Leadership

Known for his engaging presence on LinkedIn (25,000+ followers), Dr. Pandey regularly shares insights on agriculture technology, career guidance, and sustainable farming. His blog, GlobalAgriBiz, features articles in both Hindi and English, making agricultural knowledge accessible to diverse audiences.

The Man Behind the Mission

Beyond credentials and accolades, Dr. Pandey is a mentor, coach, and climate-change actor dedicated to India's agricultural future. His hands-on approach—from research fields to farmer doorsteps—embodies Ananya Seeds' commitment to quality, integrity, and innovation.

"Food security and better lives for farmers aren't just goals—they're our responsibility. Every seed we produce carries that promise."
