Leadership & Team

Meet the people behind Ananya Seeds — a team of scientists, agronomists, and industry leaders committed to delivering climate-resilient, high-yielding hybrid seeds for sustainable agriculture.

Featured Leader

Dr. Lakshmikant Pandey

Managing Director & CEO | "The Seedman"

With over 35 years of experience in seed science and agribusiness, Dr. Pandey leads Ananya Seeds with a clear focus on innovation, farmer prosperity, and climate-smart breeding. His leadership has driven the company's growth into a trusted provider of hybrid seeds across diverse agro-climatic regions.

"Our purpose is to deliver seeds that translate to stable incomes for farmers while protecting the environment."
