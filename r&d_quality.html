Research & Development

Advancing agricultural innovation through rigorous science, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to quality excellence.

Our R&D Journey: From Concept to Farm

01. Germplasm Collection

We source and preserve diverse plant germplasm from across India and globally, building a rich genetic library. This foundation enables us to identify superior traits for hybrid development.

02. Hybrid Breeding

Using advanced breeding techniques and scientific selection, we create hybrid combinations that combine desirable traits: high yield, disease resistance, stress tolerance, and superior quality.

03. Multi-Location Testing

New hybrids are tested across diverse agro-climatic zones—from coastal regions to arid zones—ensuring they perform consistently under varied conditions farmers face.

04. Laboratory Quality Assurance

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing for germination rate, purity, vigor, and pathogen screening, ensuring only premium seeds reach farmers.

05. Farmer Feedback & Optimization

We continuously gather feedback from farmer partners to refine our hybrids. This farmer-centric approach ensures our seeds deliver real-world results and consistent profitability.

Quality Assurance Standards

verified

Germination Testing

We test every seed lot for germination rate to guarantee minimum 85%+ viability. Seeds that fail quality threshold are discarded.

shield_check

Pathogen Screening

Advanced molecular and biological tests detect seed-borne pathogens. Only disease-free seeds proceed to farmer distribution.

scale

Purity Analysis

We verify genetic purity and freedom from admixture through laboratory analysis and field observation, ensuring true-to-type hybrids.

local_fire_department

Vigor Testing

Cold and accelerated aging tests ensure seeds maintain vigor during storage and transportation to reach farmers in peak condition.

visibility

Visual Inspection

Every seed bag undergoes visual quality inspection to detect discoloration, damage, or foreign material before sealing.

bug_report

Pest Detection

Fumigation and storage pest monitoring protocols ensure seeds remain pest-free throughout the entire supply chain.

Rigorous Testing & Compliance

Our state-of-the-art testing facilities and protocols exceed industry standards. We maintain compliance with all national seed regulations and international quality benchmarks.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified - Quality Management System
  • Seed Act Compliance - 100% adherence to Indian seed regulations
  • Laboratory Accreditation - NABL-approved testing facility
  • International Standards - ISTA & AOSA protocol compliance
  • Traceability Systems - Complete seed batch tracking from production to delivery
  • Regular Audits - Third-party audits ensure continuous quality improvement
